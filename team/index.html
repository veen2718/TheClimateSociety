Our Team

Richard Wang

Co-Founder, Co-President

Richard is a senior at Magee Secondary School. Since a young age, Richard has possessed an unwavering commitment to addressing environmental challenges on both a local and international scale. Witnessing the impacts of climate change firsthand on the city of Vancouver, Richard wanted to create tangible change. Thus, he created The Climate Society. As an experienced leader, Richard has displayed visionary leadership, steering communities towards impactful initiatives and fostering a culture of environmental consciousness throughout different schools and neighbourhoods. Outside of The Climate Society, Richard is a nationally-ranked squash athlete and has also led Team Canada at both the 2022 & 2023 International History Olympiads.

Otavio Nunes

Co-Founder, Co-President

Otavio's unique journey from Brazil to Canada and currently residing in Switzerland at Leysin American School has fueled his passion for environmental conservation. Jayden Vo

Vice-President

Jayden Vo is a grade 12 student currently attending Magee Secondary School. As Vice President of the Climate Society, Jayden constantly strives for environmental change and hopes for a more sustainable and healthy ecosystem in the years to come. From removing invasive plants at Heywood Park to partaking in voluntary city-wide cleanups around Vancouver, Jayden constantly shows his dedication towards a greener planet. On his own time, Jayden regularly participates in beach cleanups down at Kitsilano Beach, picking up anything that may be environmentally harmful. From garbage in the ocean to rotting food in the sand, he ensures that the beach is left spotless after he has finished. Jayden loves spreading the message of creating a more sustainable environment to his friends and family as he is eager to see his vision of a greener planet become a reality.
